## âœ¨ Animation Requirements
- **Entrance**: Fade-in/up on page load with stagger for lists/grids.
- **Scroll-Triggered**: Use Framer Motion's useInView for reveals (e.g., slide from left/right, scale up).
- **Hover/Interactions**: Scale, opacity changes, and icon bounces with spring physics.
- **Transitions**: Smooth easing (e.g., easeInOut); modals with scale-from-center.
- **Parallax**: Subtle background shifts on scroll (GSAP if complex).
